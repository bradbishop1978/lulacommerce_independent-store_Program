FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before signing up.

What is Lula Commerce?+
Lula Commerce makes delivery easy for independent store operators by consolidating DoorDash, Uber Eats, and Grubhub into one system — the Lula app. You don't have to manage multiple apps, menus, or support issues. Lula handles all of that for you — updates, fixes, outages, customer issues, everything — so your staff can just accept orders and stay focused on the store.
Why not just sign up with DoorDash or Uber Eats directly?+
You can — but managing each marketplace separately means a different tablet, different operational requirements, and different systems for updating your menu and pricing. Lula's operators consistently say that working with each platform independently becomes unmanageable fast. Lula takes all of that complexity away: you work with us, and we publish your inventory and pricing to all channels while bringing every order into one simple interface.
What if I'm already on DoorDash or Uber Eats?+
Perfect — Lula will consolidate your existing marketplace presence into a single dashboard so you stop managing multiple apps. You'll also gain access to any marketplaces you're not yet on, and Lula handles all the ongoing management across every channel. Many of our operators come to us already active on one or two platforms and find that Lula saves them significant time while increasing their total order volume.
Do I need extra staff to manage delivery orders?+
No. That's exactly the problem Lula solves. All marketplace orders come into a single tablet, so your existing team accepts them in one place — the same way you'd handle any other order. Lula's team handles refunds, disputes, menu issues, and marketplace communications on your behalf, 24/7. You don't need a dedicated digital commerce manager.
Can I mark up my prices to cover delivery costs?+
Yes. You can designate a markup on your delivery menu, and most consumers expect to pay a small premium for items delivered. This is a common practice among Lula's operators and an effective way to maintain your margins while still offering competitive delivery pricing.
Can I sell age-restricted items through delivery?+
In most areas, yes. Delivery programs follow state, county, and local laws. The delivery service providers (Uber Eats, DoorDash) handle ID verification and dispatch only qualified drivers for deliveries of age-restricted items. Lula supports age-restricted item configurations in your menu setup.
How does my menu get set up and updated?+
To get you live fast, Lula starts with a prebuilt delivery menu configured for your store. Once you're live, you're free to adjust pricing and submit additional items to be added. Changes sync automatically across all marketplaces — you don't need to update each platform separately. Lula's team handles the technical work; you just tell us what you want.
How do I get paid, and how often?+
Each marketplace (DoorDash, Uber Eats, Grubhub) remits funds directly to you, less their standard fees, every week. Lula does not hold your funds — payouts come directly from each platform on their regular weekly schedule. You'll set up your deposit account during onboarding so everything is wired correctly from day one.
What is the 10% revenue share?+
The 10% revenue share is the fee for Lula's managed AI services — the automated systems running on your behalf around the clock: order routing, menu syncing across all marketplaces, uptime monitoring, dispute resolution, and more. It's only charged on delivery orders you actually receive, which means we only earn when you do.
What happens after the 90 days?+
After 90 days, the $19/month fee kicks in per location. The 10% revenue share is already active from when you went live — that part doesn't change. You'll receive a reminder as your 90-day period ends, and monthly billing begins automatically. There's no long-term contract — you can cancel at any time.
How long does it take to get set up?+
Most stores are live within one week of signing up. The sign-up process itself takes about 5 minutes. Lula handles the menu configuration and marketplace setup on your behalf. Once your tablet arrives, connecting and going live takes about 15 minutes. Lula's managed-services team coordinates everything — you don't need technical expertise.
How do I get started?+
Click the button below or visit onboarding.lulacommerce.com/gen to complete your sign-up form. You'll receive an email with next steps — including Stripe billing setup, deposit account configuration, and store info collection. If you have questions at any point, visit our Help Center or reach out to your account team.
